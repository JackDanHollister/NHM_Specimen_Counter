How To Use

Quick steps to run a count:

  1. Click the upload area and pick up to 10 photos (JPG/PNG). Keep drawers clear and well-lit.
  2. Choose a model from the dropdown. If you’re unsure, leave the default selected.
  3. Press “Count Specimens”. Wait while the model runs.
  4. When done, click “View Results” to download the zip with annotated images and summary files.
Need another model? Email to request new models for your specimens.
